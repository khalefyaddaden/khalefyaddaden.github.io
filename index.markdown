I am a visiting fellow as a member of the team *Algebra, Representations and Topology* ([ART](https://irma.math.unistra.fr/teams/art.html)) at the *Institut de Recherche Mathématique Avancée*, ([IRMA](http://irma.math.unistra.fr)) Laboratory in Strasbourg (France). 

I have defended my thesis under on March 14<sup>th</sup> 2023 under the supervision of *Benjamin Enriquez*. My thesis is entitled "*Algebraic structures associated to the double shuffle relations between multiple polylogarithm values at roots of unity*". The manuscript is available [here](https://theses.hal.science/tel-04017713).

**Keywords**: Multiple polylogarithm values; Double Shuffle; Harmonic coproduct; Hopf algebra; Torsor; Crossed product algebra; Filtered algebra.

My area of interest is multiple zeta values and their cyclotomic generalisation (multiple polylogarithm values at roots of unity) alonside related topics.
